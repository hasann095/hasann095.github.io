about me

I'm Hasan, and *SPOILER ALERT*,
I love design.

My love for design started when I designed a user interface for a futuristic washing machine.

Quite frankly, I never expected to find myself as someone who would enjoy design. My only experience with "design", or at least what I thought of it as a kid, was creating houses and stick figures on MS Paint. It wasn't until college when I was really exposed to what design truly meant. My first ever design project was to create a user interface for a washing machine. As lame as that sounds, it challenged me. It made me think of the way I used to interact with my washing machine, the various pain points I used to feel, and how I wanted to improve them but couldn't. It was that time I realized that a washing machine wasn't the only device that I felt difficulty using sometimes, and also that I was not the only one who felt difficulty using different devices.

Since then, I've been taking out the time to work on various design projects, some being work or school related, and others as a result of a general passion to make things look and feel better. You'll usually find me at a cafe working on a passion project, or just redesigning something that already exists.
