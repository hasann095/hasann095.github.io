Exit Ticket Redesign

This is a branding and document design project, where I was tasked with the redesign of an Exit Ticket template to be used in the classroom by a Carnegie Mellon Professor. Over a period of a few months, I designed a template that was then implemented for use in the classroom, and was presented as part of the professor's research project at Carnegie Mellon's "Meeting of the Minds" national research symposium and other local and international events.

Role
